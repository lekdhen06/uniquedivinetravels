National bird

Raven is the national bird of Bhutan. Legend has it that the Raven sits atop the crown of the king.

National animal

Legend has it that the Takin is a combination of a goat and a yak.

National flower

Blue poppy if you are pondering about it.

National game

Archery is the national game of Bhutan.

National food

Ema-datshi - as the locals call it - is the national food.

National dress

Gho and kira is the national dress of the country.
